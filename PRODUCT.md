# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Thai primary school students (Grade 3-6) learning Computing Science, and their teachers who manage content and track progress.

## Product Purpose
A premium educational platform that makes learning computing science fun, interactive, and accessible for Thai children through digital lessons, games, and quizzes.

## Brand Personality
Friendly, Energetic, and Modern. It should feel like a playground for learning, not a digital textbook.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered and complex LMS interfaces (e.g., Moodle default themes).
- Boring, spreadsheet-like data tables for teachers.
- Low-contrast or tiny typography that is hard for children to read.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity**: Use large, clear UI elements and simple navigation paths.
- **Visual Delight**: Incorporate micro-animations, vibrant (but balanced) colors, and rounded shapes to reduce friction.
- **Content-First**: Ensure learning materials are the focus, with minimal UI distractions.
- **Inclusive Design**: Prioritize Thai typography legibility and accessible color contrast.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- High legibility for Noto Sans Thai.
- Large touch targets for young users.
